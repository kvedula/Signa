On the Phone. and Watch.

  • Play Matches

    Either start or join a match with your friends and play for as long as you want.

  • Unique Maps

    You create and set the bounds of where you want to play.

  • Chat

    An in-app messager lets players communicate with everyone playing the current game at any point in time.

  • Group Play

    After all, Hide & Seek is a multiplayer game, so it would not work if it was a single player game.

  • Spectator

    Spectators are allowed to view the game go on in real time as the hiders and seeker do their thing.

  • Highly Scalable

    The game does not have to be people running around. It can involve cars, or any type of transportation, even airplanes if you are rich enough.

  • Camera

    Take pictures while playing the game. See the seeker while hiding and they dont see you. Take a selfie.

  • Health

    Track all your health data and see how fit your are!
